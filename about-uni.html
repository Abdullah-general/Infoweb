Cihan University - Erbil

Cihan University - Erbil is a leading private university in the Kurdistan region of Iraq. Established in 2007, it offers a variety of academic programs accredited by the Ministry of Higher Education in Iraq and the Kurdistan Region.

Academic Departments

  • Engineering
  • Computer Science
  • Media
  • Law
  • Business Administration
  • Health Sciences
  • Arts and Humanities

University Goals

  • Provide an integrated educational environment that encourages creativity and innovation.
  • Prepare graduates to compete in both local and international job markets.
  • Contribute to the development of scientific research and community service.

University Statistics

  • Over 18,000 students.
  • 320 faculty members.
  • 128 classrooms and laboratories.
  • 27 specializations.
